APD System

An Automatic Polyp Detection (APD) system is a computer-based solution designed to automatically identify and detect colonic polyps in medical images, particularly those obtained during colonoscopy procedures.

The primary objective of an APD system is to assist healthcare professionals, such as gastroenterologists, in detecting polyps more efficiently and accurately during colorectal cancer screening.
